The Affordable Care Act


A Brief Introduction and Timeline

More commonly known as Obamacare, the Affordable Care Act (ACA) is one of the most important pieces of health care legislation since the creation of Medicaid and Medicare in 1965.

However, despite this law's significance, there seems to be a large amount of misunderstanding around what it is, what it aims to do, or even when it happened. Rumors and misconceptions have been circulating about the law since before it was even being drafted.
